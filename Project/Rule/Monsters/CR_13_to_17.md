# Monsters — CR 13 to 17 (XP 10,000–18,000)

> Major bosses. Campaign-defining threats cho party level 10-17.

---

## Beholder (Mắt Quỷ) (CR 13, 10,000 XP)
- Large Aberration | Lawful Evil
- **AC** 18 (Natural) | **HP** 180 (19d10+76) | **Speed** 0 ft, Fly 20 ft (hover)
- **STR** 10 (+0) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 18 (+4) **INT** 17 (+3) **WIS** 15 (+2) **CHA** 17 (+3)
- **Saves** INT +8, WIS +7, CHA +8
- **Skills** Perception +12
- **Immunities** Prone
- **Senses** Darkvision 120 ft, PP 22
- **Languages** Deep Speech, Undercommon
- **Traits:**
  - **Antimagic Cone.** 150ft cone từ central eye. Khu vực = Antimagic Field. Beholder chọn hướng mỗi turn
- **Actions:**
  - **Bite.** +5, 14 (4d6) Piercing
  - **Eye Rays.** 3 random rays (120ft). Mỗi ray nhắm 1 target khác:
    1. **Charm Ray.** DC 16 WIS → Charmed 1h
    2. **Paralyzing Ray.** DC 16 CON → Paralyzed 1 min
    3. **Fear Ray.** DC 16 WIS → Frightened 1 min
    4. **Slowing Ray.** DC 16 DEX → Speed halved + no Reactions + 1 action or BA
    5. **Enervation Ray.** DC 16 CON → 36 (8d8) Necrotic (½ save)
    6. **Telekinetic Ray.** DC 16 STR → Move 30ft. Object → fling 30ft, 1 creature: 10 (3d6) Bludgeoning
    7. **Sleep Ray.** DC 16 WIS → fall asleep 1 min
    8. **Petrification Ray.** DC 16 DEX → Restrained → next fail = **Petrified**
    9. **Disintegration Ray.** DC 16 DEX → 45 (10d8) Force. 0 HP = **dust**
    10. **Death Ray.** DC 16 DEX → 55 (10d10) Necrotic. 0 HP = **die**
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Eye Ray.** 1 eye ray

> *⚠ ICONIC boss monster. Antimagic Cone shuts down casters. Death Ray + Disintegration + Petrification = TPK potential. Plan CAREFULLY.*

## Adult Red Dragon (Rồng Đỏ Trưởng Thành) (CR 17, 18,000 XP)
- Huge Dragon | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 19 (Natural) | **HP** 256 (19d12+133) | **Speed** 40 ft, Climb 40 ft, Fly 80 ft
- **STR** 27 (+8) **DEX** 10 (+0) **CON** 25 (+7) **INT** 16 (+3) **WIS** 13 (+1) **CHA** 21 (+5)
- **Saves** DEX +6, CON +13, WIS +7, CHA +11
- **Skills** Perception +13, Stealth +6
- **Immunities** Fire
- **Senses** Blindsight 60 ft, Darkvision 120 ft, PP 23
- **Languages** Common, Draconic
- **Traits:**
  - **Legendary Resistance (3/day).** Fail save → choose to succeed
  - **Frightful Presence.** 120ft, DC 19 WIS → Frightened 1 min
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** Frightful Presence + 3 attacks (1 Bite + 2 Claws)
  - **Bite.** +14, 19 (2d10+8) Piercing + 7 (2d6) Fire
  - **Claw.** +14, 15 (2d6+8) Slashing
  - **Tail.** +14, 17 (2d8+8) Bludgeoning
  - **Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6).** 60ft cone, DC 21 DEX, **63 (18d6) Fire** (½ save)
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Detect.** WIS (Perception) check
  - **Tail Attack.** 1 tail attack
  - **Wing Attack (2).** 15ft, DC 22 DEX → 15 (2d6+8) Bludgeoning + Prone. Dragon flies up to ½ speed

> *THE quintessential D&D boss. 18d6 Fire Breath = avg 63 damage. Legendary Resistance = can't be locked down. Wing Attack = repositioning.*

## Lich (Pháp Sư Xác Ướp) (CR 21, 33,000 XP)
- Medium Undead | Any Evil
- **AC** 17 (Natural) | **HP** 135 (18d8+54) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 11 (+0) **DEX** 16 (+3) **CON** 16 (+3) **INT** 20 (+5) **WIS** 14 (+2) **CHA** 16 (+3)
- **Saves** CON +10, INT +12, WIS +9
- **Skills** Arcana +18, History +12, Insight +9, Perception +9
- **Resistances** Cold, Lightning, Necrotic
- **Immunities** Poison, Necrotic; B/P/S nonmagical; Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened, Paralyzed, Poisoned
- **Senses** Truesight 120 ft, PP 19
- **Languages** Common + 5 others
- **Traits:**
  - **Legendary Resistance (3/day).**
  - **Rejuvenation.** Có phylactery → trở lại sống 1d10 days. Destroy phylactery = permanent death
  - **Turn Resistance.** Advantage saves vs Turn Undead
- **Spellcasting (INT, DC 20, +12):**
  - Cantrips: Mage Hand, Prestidigitation, Ray of Frost
  - 1st-9th: Full Wizard slots. Key: Shield, Counterspell, Fireball, Finger of Death, **Power Word Kill** (9th)
- **Actions:**
  - **Paralyzing Touch.** +12 melee, 10 (3d6) Cold. DC 18 CON → **Paralyzed** 1 min
  - **Frightening Gaze.** 1 creature 60ft, DC 18 WIS → Frightened 1 min
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Cantrip.** Cast cantrip
  - **Paralyzing Touch (2).** Use Paralyzing Touch
  - **Frightening Gaze (2).** Use Frightening Gaze
  - **Disrupt Life (3).** Living creatures 20ft: DC 18 CON → 21 (6d6) Necrotic. Lich heals equal

> *⚠ CAMPAIGN VILLAIN. Power Word Kill = no save, 100 HP or less = die. Phylactery must be found + destroyed. DC 20 spellcasting = near impossible to save.*

## Vampire (Ma Cà Rồng) (CR 13, 10,000 XP)
- Medium Undead (Shapechanger) | Lawful Evil
- **AC** 16 (Natural) | **HP** 144 (17d8+68) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 18 (+4) **DEX** 18 (+4) **CON** 18 (+4) **INT** 17 (+3) **WIS** 15 (+2) **CHA** 18 (+4)
- **Saves** DEX +9, WIS +7, CHA +9
- **Skills** Perception +7, Stealth +9
- **Resistances** Necrotic; B/P/S nonmagical
- **Traits:**
  - **Shapechanger.** Bat (Tiny, Fly 30), Mist (Fly 20, can't attack), hoặc True Form
  - **Legendary Resistance (3/day).**
  - **Misty Escape.** 0 HP (not sunlight/running water) → mist, fly to coffin, revive 1h
  - **Regeneration.** 20 HP/turn (stops sunlight/running water)
  - **Spider Climb.** Climb bất kỳ surface
  - **Vampire Weaknesses:** Forbiddance (can't enter without invite), Harmed by Running Water (20 Acid/turn), Stake to Heart (Paralyzed khi staked in coffin), Sunlight Hypersensitivity (20 Radiant/turn + Disadvantage)
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack (Vampire form).** 2 attacks, max 1 Bite
  - **Unarmed Strike.** +9, 8 (1d8+4) Bludgeoning. Grapple DC 18
  - **Bite (vs willing/grappled/restrained/incapacitated).** +9, 7 (1d6+4) Piercing + 10 (3d6) Necrotic. **Max HP reduced**. 0 = die → rise as Vampire Spawn
  - **Charm.** DC 17 WIS → Charmed. Willing source of bites. 24h or damage breaks
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Move.** Move without OA
  - **Unarmed Strike.** 1 attack
  - **Bite (2).** 1 Bite (must have creature grappled)

> *Regen 20/turn + Misty Escape = essentially unkillable until you find the coffin. Disable with sunlight/running water/stake.*

## Death Knight (CR 17, 18,000 XP)
- Medium Undead | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 20 (Plate + Shield) | **HP** 180 (19d8+95) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 20 (+5) **DEX** 11 (+0) **CON** 20 (+5) **INT** 12 (+1) **WIS** 16 (+3) **CHA** 18 (+4)
- **Saves** DEX +6, WIS +9, CHA +10
- **Immunities** Necrotic, Poison; Exhaustion, Frightened, Poisoned
- **Senses** Darkvision 120 ft, PP 13
- **Languages** Abyssal, Common
- **Traits:**
  - **Magic Resistance.** Advantage saves vs spells
  - **Marshal Undead.** Undead 60ft: Advantage saves vs Turn
  - **Spellcasting (CHA, DC 18, +10).** 1st-5th level Paladin slots. Key: Command, Compelled Duel, Banishing Smite, Destructive Wave, Staggering Smite
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** 3 Longsword
  - **Longsword.** +11, 9 (1d8+5) Slashing + 18 (4d8) Necrotic
  - **Hellfire Orb (1/day).** 20ft radius 60ft range. DC 18 DEX, 35 (10d6) Fire + 35 (10d6) Necrotic (½ save)
- **Reactions:**
  - **Parry.** +6 AC vs 1 melee attack
- **Legendary Actions:** None nhưng vẫn rất mạnh

> *Anti-Paladin boss. AC 20 + Magic Resistance + 4d8 Necrotic on every hit + Hellfire Orb AoE. Brutal melee combatant.*
