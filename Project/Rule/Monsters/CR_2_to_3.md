# Monsters — CR 2 to 3 (XP 450–700)

> Boss encounters cho party level 1-4. Threats solo hoặc nhóm nhỏ cho level 3-5.

---

## Ogre (Quái Vật Khổng Lồ) (CR 2, 450 XP)
- Large Giant | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 11 (Hide) | **HP** 59 (7d10+21) | **Speed** 40 ft
- **STR** 19 (+4) **DEX** 8 (−1) **CON** 16 (+3) **INT** 5 (−3) **WIS** 7 (−2) **CHA** 7 (−2)
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 8
- **Languages** Common, Giant
- **Actions:**
  - **Greatclub.** +6, 13 (2d8+4) Bludgeoning
  - **Javelin.** +6, range 30/120, 11 (2d6+4) Piercing

> *Simple bruiser. AC thấp nhưng HP cao + damage mạnh. Good encounter solo Lv2-3.*

## Ghast (CR 2, 450 XP)
- Medium Undead | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 13 | **HP** 36 (8d8) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 16 (+3) **DEX** 17 (+3) **CON** 10 (+0) **INT** 11 (+0) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 8 (−1)
- **Resistances** Necrotic
- **Immunities** Poison; Charmed, Exhaustion, Poisoned
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 10
- **Traits:**
  - **Stench.** Creatures 5ft: DC 10 CON → Poisoned đến start of next turn
  - **Turning Defiance.** Advantage saves vs Turn Undead (cho mình + undead 30ft)
- **Actions:**
  - **Bite.** +3, 12 (2d8+3) Piercing
  - **Claws.** +5, 10 (2d6+3) Slashing. DC 10 CON → **Paralyzed** 1 min

> *Ghoul nâng cấp. Stench AoE + Paralysis = nhóm Ghast + Ghouls rất nguy hiểm.*

## Mimic (Quái Vật Ngụy Trang) (CR 2, 450 XP)
- Medium Monstrosity (Shapechanger) | Neutral
- **AC** 12 (Natural) | **HP** 58 (9d8+18) | **Speed** 15 ft
- **STR** 17 (+3) **DEX** 12 (+1) **CON** 15 (+2) **INT** 5 (−3) **WIS** 13 (+1) **CHA** 8 (−1)
- **Skills** Stealth +5
- **Immunities** Acid, Prone
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 11
- **Traits:**
  - **Shapechanger.** Biến thành object hoặc về dạng gốc
  - **Adhesive (Object Form).** Dính vào creature chạm → Grappled (DC 13)
  - **False Appearance.** Trông y hệt object thật khi ở object form
- **Actions:**
  - **Pseudopod.** +5, 7 (1d8+3) Bludgeoning. Dính → Grappled (DC 13)
  - **Bite.** +5, 7 (1d8+3) Piercing + 4 (1d8) Acid

> *Classic trap monster. Chest → CHOMP! Player paranoia guaranteed.*

## Werewolf (Người Sói) (CR 3, 700 XP)
- Medium Humanoid (Human, Shapechanger) | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 11 (Natural, 12 wolf/hybrid) | **HP** 58 (9d8+18) | **Speed** 30 ft (40 wolf)
- **STR** 15 (+2) **DEX** 13 (+1) **CON** 14 (+2) **INT** 10 (+0) **WIS** 11 (+0) **CHA** 10 (+0)
- **Skills** Perception +4, Stealth +3
- **Immunities** B/P/S from nonmagical/non-silvered weapons
- **Senses** PP 14
- **Languages** Common (can't speak in wolf form)
- **Traits:**
  - **Shapechanger.** BA: human/wolf/hybrid
  - **Keen Hearing and Smell.** Advantage Perception
- **Actions (Hybrid):**
  - **Multiattack.** 2 attacks (1 can be bite)
  - **Bite (wolf/hybrid).** +4, 6 (1d8+2) Piercing. DC 12 CON → **cursed with lycanthropy**
  - **Claws (hybrid).** +4, 7 (2d4+2) Slashing

> *Immunity nonmagical weapons = GAME CHANGER ở low levels. Cần magic/silver weapons.*

## Manticore (CR 3, 700 XP)

- **AC** 14 (Natural) | **HP** 68 (8d10+24) | **Speed** 30 ft, Fly 50 ft
- **STR** 17 (+3) **DEX** 16 (+3) **CON** 17 (+3) **INT** 7 (−2) **WIS** 12 (+1) **CHA** 8 (−1)
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 11
- **Languages** Common
- **Traits:**
  - **Tail Spike Regrowth.** 24 spikes, mọc lại sau Long Rest
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** 3 attacks: 1 Bite + 2 Claws, HOẶC 3 Tail Spikes
  - **Bite.** +5, 7 (1d8+3) Piercing
  - **Claw.** +5, 6 (1d6+3) Slashing
  - **Tail Spike.** +5, range 100/200, 7 (1d8+3) Piercing

> *Flying ranged attacker. 3 Tail Spikes từ xa = kite melee party. Trí tuệ: có thể đàm phán.*

## Gelatinous Cube (Khối Thạch) (CR 2, 450 XP)
- Large Ooze | Unaligned
- **AC** 6 | **HP** 84 (8d10+40) | **Speed** 15 ft
- **STR** 14 (+2) **DEX** 3 (−4) **CON** 20 (+5) **INT** 1 (−5) **WIS** 6 (−2) **CHA** 1 (−5)
- **Immunities** Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Exhaustion, Frightened, Prone
- **Senses** Blindsight 60 ft (blind beyond), PP 8
- **Traits:**
  - **Ooze Cube.** Fills 10ft cube. Creatures can enter. DC 12 DEX to avoid
  - **Transparent.** DC 15 Perception to notice. Surprise vào nó
- **Actions:**
  - **Pseudopod.** +4, 10 (3d6) Acid
  - **Engulf.** Move over creatures. DC 12 DEX → Engulfed (restrained, 21 (6d6) Acid/turn)

> *Classic dungeon hazard. Transparent = walk INTO it. 6d6 Acid/turn khi bị nuốt!*
