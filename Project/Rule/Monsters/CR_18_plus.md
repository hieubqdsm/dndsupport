## Ancient Red Dragon (Rồng Đỏ Cổ Đại) (CR 24, 62,000 XP)
- Gargantuan Dragon | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 22 (Natural) | **HP** 546 (28d20+252) | **Speed** 40 ft, Climb 40 ft, Fly 80 ft
- **STR** 30 (+10) **DEX** 10 (+0) **CON** 29 (+9) **INT** 18 (+4) **WIS** 15 (+2) **CHA** 23 (+6)
- **Saves** DEX +7, CON +16, WIS +9, CHA +13
- **Skills** Perception +16, Stealth +7
- **Immunities** Fire
- **Senses** Blindsight 60 ft, Darkvision 120 ft, PP 26
- **Languages** Common, Draconic
- **Traits:**
  - **Legendary Resistance (3/day).**
  - **Frightful Presence.** 120ft, DC 21 WIS → Frightened 1 min
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** Frightful Presence + 3 attacks (1 Bite + 2 Claws)
  - **Bite.** +17, 21 (2d10+10) Piercing + 14 (4d6) Fire
  - **Claw.** +17, 17 (2d6+10) Slashing
  - **Tail.** +17, 19 (2d8+10) Bludgeoning
  - **Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6).** 90ft cone, DC 24 DEX, **91 (26d6) Fire** (½ save)
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Detect.** WIS (Perception) check
  - **Tail Attack.** 1 tail attack
  - **Wing Attack (2).** 15ft, DC 25 DEX → 17 (2d6+10) Bludgeoning + Prone. Fly ½ speed

> *⚠ 546 HP. 26d6 Fire Breath = avg 91 damage. +17 attack. DC 24/25 saves. The ultimate dragon.*

## Tarrasque (CR 30, 155,000 XP)
- Gargantuan Monstrosity (Titan) | Unaligned
- **AC** 25 (Natural) | **HP** 676 (33d20+330) | **Speed** 40 ft
- **STR** 30 (+10) **DEX** 11 (+0) **CON** 30 (+10) **INT** 3 (−4) **WIS** 11 (+0) **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Saves** INT +5, WIS +9, CHA +9
- **Immunities** Fire, Poison; Charmed, Frightened, Paralyzed, Poisoned
- **Senses** Blindsight 120 ft, PP 10
- **Traits:**
  - **Legendary Resistance (3/day).**
  - **Magic Resistance.** Advantage saves vs spells
  - **Reflective Carapace.** Magic Missile/line spells: **reflected** (random new target). 1-5 on d6 for other targeted spells = reflected
  - **Siege Monster.** Double damage vs objects/structures
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** 5 attacks: 1 Bite + 2 Claws + 1 Horns + 1 Tail. Can use Swallow instead of Bite
  - **Bite.** +19, 36 (4d12+10) Piercing. Grapple DC 20. Medium or smaller → Swallow
  - **Claw.** +19, 28 (4d8+10) Slashing
  - **Horns.** +19, 32 (4d10+10) Piercing
  - **Tail.** +19, 24 (4d6+10) Bludgeoning. DC 20 STR → Prone
  - **Swallow.** Blinded, Restrained, 56 (16d6) Acid/turn. 60+ damage from inside → regurgitate
  - **Frightful Presence.** 120ft, DC 17 WIS → Frightened 1 min
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Attack.** 1 Claw or Tail
  - **Move.** Move ½ speed
  - **Chomp (2).** 1 Bite. Grappled → Swallow

> *⚠ THE ULTIMATE MONSTER. 676 HP. AC 25. +19 attacks. 5 attacks/turn. Reflects spells. 16d6 Acid/turn Swallow. Requires entire high-level party + strategy.*

## Kraken (CR 23, 50,000 XP)
- Gargantuan Monstrosity (Titan) | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 18 (Natural) | **HP** 472 (27d20+189) | **Speed** 20 ft, Swim 60 ft
- **STR** 30 (+10) **DEX** 11 (+0) **CON** 25 (+7) **INT** 22 (+6) **WIS** 18 (+4) **CHA** 20 (+5)
- **Saves** STR +17, DEX +7, CON +14, INT +13, WIS +11
- **Immunities** Lightning; B/P/S nonmagical; Frightened, Paralyzed
- **Senses** Truesight 120 ft, PP 14
- **Languages** Understands all, Telepathy 120 ft
- **Traits:**
  - **Amphibious.** Air and water
  - **Freedom of Movement.** Ignore terrain/restraints
  - **Siege Monster.** Double damage vs objects
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** 3 Tentacle. Can replace 1 with Fling
  - **Bite.** +17, 23 (3d8+10) Piercing. Swallow on hit (DC 23). 42 (12d6) Acid/turn inside
  - **Tentacle.** +17, 20 (3d6+10) Bludgeoning. Grapple (DC 18). 10ft reach
  - **Fling.** Fling grappled creature 60ft → 13 (3d8) Bludgeoning + Prone
  - **Lightning Storm.** 3 bolts, 120ft, DC 23 DEX, 22 (4d10) Lightning each
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Tentacle Attack or Fling.** 1 attack
  - **Lightning Storm (2).** Use Lightning Storm
  - **Ink Cloud (3).** 60ft radius heavily obscured, water becomes poisonous

> *Ocean boss. Lightning Storm + multiattack tentacles. Swallow = 12d6/turn. Ink Cloud = escape/trap.*
