# Monsters — CR ⅛ to ¼ (XP 25–50)

> Minions, scouts, nhóm nhỏ enemies. Dùng theo đám (3-8 con) chống party level 1-2.

---

## Kobold (CR ⅛, 25 XP)
- Small Humanoid (Kobold) | Lawful Evil
- **AC** 12 (Leather) | **HP** 5 (2d6 − 2) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 7 (−2) **DEX** 15 (+2) **CON** 9 (−1) **INT** 8 (−1) **WIS** 7 (−2) **CHA** 8 (−1)
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 8
- **Languages** Common, Draconic
- **Traits:**
  - **Pack Tactics.** Advantage attack nếu ally trong 5ft mục tiêu
  - **Sunlight Sensitivity.** Disadvantage attack & Perception khi sunlight
- **Actions:**
  - **Dagger.** +4, 4 (1d4+2) Piercing
  - **Sling.** +4, range 30/120, 4 (1d4+2) Bludgeoning

> *Luôn dùng theo nhóm 4-6. Pack Tactics = nguy hiểm hơn CR suggests.*

- **Actions:**

> *Undead cơ bản. Vulnerable to Bludgeoning = mace/hammer hiệu quả gấp đôi.*

## Giant Spider (Nhện Khổng Lồ) (CR ¼, 50 XP)
- Large Beast | Unaligned
- **AC** 14 (Natural) | **HP** 26 (4d10 + 4) | **Speed** 30 ft, Climb 30 ft
- **STR** 14 (+2) **DEX** 16 (+3) **CON** 12 (+1) **INT** 2 (−4) **WIS** 11 (+0) **CHA** 4 (−3)
- **Skills** Stealth +7
- **Senses** Blindsight 10 ft, Darkvision 60 ft, PP 10
- **Traits:**
  - **Spider Climb.** Climb khó surfaces + ceilings
  - **Web Sense.** Biết vị trí creatures trên web
  - **Web Walker.** Ignore web movement restrictions
- **Actions:**
  - **Bite.** +5, 7 (1d8+3) Piercing + DC 11 CON → 9 (2d8) Poison (½ on save)
  - **Web (Recharge 5-6).** +5 ranged 30/60, Large or smaller → Restrained. DC 12 STR to break, hoặc AC 10 HP 5

> *Stealth +7! Ambush hunter. Web = restrain rất annoying ở level thấp.*

## Sprite (CR ¼, 50 XP)
- Tiny Fey | Neutral Good
- **AC** 15 (Leather) | **HP** 2 (1d4) | **Speed** 10 ft, Fly 40 ft
- **STR** 3 (−4) **DEX** 18 (+4) **CON** 10 (+0) **INT** 14 (+2) **WIS** 13 (+1) **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Skills** Perception +3, Stealth +8
- **Senses** PP 13
- **Languages** Common, Elvish, Sylvan
- **Actions:**
  - **Longsword.** +2, 1 Slashing
  - **Shortbow.** +6, range 40/160, 1 Piercing + DC 10 CON → Poisoned 1 min. Fail 5+ → Unconscious
  - **Heart Sight.** Touch creature → biết alignment + emotional state
  - **Invisibility.** Invisible until attack/cast

> *Stealth +8! Heart Sight = biết alignment. Invisible + Poison bow = sneaky.*
