# Monsters — CR ½ to 1 (XP 100–200)

> Mối đe dọa cơ bản cho party level 1-3. Dùng đơn lẻ hoặc nhóm nhỏ.

---

## Orc (CR ½, 100 XP)
- Medium Humanoid (Orc) | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 13 (Hide) | **HP** 15 (2d8+6) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 16 (+3) **DEX** 12 (+1) **CON** 16 (+3) **INT** 7 (−2) **WIS** 11 (+0) **CHA** 10 (+0)
- **Skills** Intimidation +2
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 10
- **Languages** Common, Orc
- **Traits:**
  - **Aggressive.** BA: Dash toward hostile creature
- **Actions:**
  - **Greataxe.** +5, 9 (1d12+3) Slashing
  - **Javelin.** +5, range 30/120, 6 (1d6+3) Piercing

## Thug (Tay Côn Đồ) (CR ½, 100 XP)
- Medium Humanoid | Any non-good
- **AC** 11 (Leather) | **HP** 32 (5d8+10) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 15 (+2) **DEX** 11 (+0) **CON** 14 (+2) **INT** 10 (+0) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Skills** Intimidation +2
- **Languages** Common
- **Traits:**
  - **Pack Tactics.** Advantage attack nếu ally 5ft
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** 2 melee attacks
  - **Mace.** +4, 5 (1d6+2) Bludgeoning
  - **Heavy Crossbow.** +2, range 100/400, 5 (1d10) Piercing

> *32 HP cho CR ½ = rất tanky. Pack Tactics + Multiattack = dangerous.*

## Specter (Quỷ Bóng) (CR 1, 200 XP)
- Medium Undead | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 12 | **HP** 22 (5d8) | **Speed** 0 ft, Fly 50 ft (hover)
- **STR** 1 (−5) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 11 (+0) **INT** 10 (+0) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Resistances** Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Thunder; B/P/S nonmagical
- **Immunities** Necrotic, Poison; Charmed, Exhaustion, Grappled, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned, Prone, Restrained, Unconscious
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 10
- **Traits:**
  - **Incorporeal Movement.** Đi xuyên qua objects/creatures. 5 Force nếu kết thúc turn trong object
  - **Sunlight Sensitivity.** Disadvantage attack & Perception khi sunlight
- **Actions:**
  - **Life Drain.** +4, 10 (3d6) Necrotic. DC 10 CON → **Max HP giảm** bằng damage. Về 0 = chết.

> *Life Drain giảm MAX HP = rất nguy hiểm! Immunity list khổng lồ. Cần magic weapons.*
