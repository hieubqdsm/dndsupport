# Monsters — CR 6 to 8 (XP 2,300–3,900)

> Mid-game threats. Boss encounters cho party level 5-8.

## Mage (Pháp Sư) (CR 6, 2,300 XP)
- Medium Humanoid | Any
- **AC** 12 (15 w/ Mage Armor) | **HP** 40 (9d8) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 9 (−1) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 11 (+0) **INT** 17 (+3) **WIS** 12 (+1) **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Saves** INT +6, WIS +4
- **Skills** Arcana +6, History +6
- **Languages** Bất kỳ 4
- **Spellcasting (INT, DC 14, +6):**
  - Cantrips: Fire Bolt, Light, Mage Hand, Prestidigitation
  - 1st (4 slots): Detect Magic, Mage Armor, Magic Missile, **Shield**
  - 2nd (3 slots): **Misty Step**, Suggestion
  - 3rd (3 slots): **Counterspell**, **Fireball**, Fly
  - 4th (3 slots): **Greater Invisibility**, Ice Storm
  - 5th (1 slot): **Cone of Cold**
- **Actions:**
  - **Dagger.** +5, 4 (1d4+2) Piercing

> *HP thấp nhưng Fireball + Shield + Counterspell = scary. Kill fast trước khi cast.*

## Medusa (CR 6, 2,300 XP)
- Medium Monstrosity | Lawful Evil
- **AC** 15 (Natural) | **HP** 127 (17d8+51) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 10 (+0) **DEX** 15 (+2) **CON** 16 (+3) **INT** 12 (+1) **WIS** 13 (+1) **CHA** 15 (+2)
- **Skills** Deception +5, Insight +4, Perception +4, Stealth +5
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 14
- **Languages** Common
- **Traits:**
  - **Petrifying Gaze.** Creatures 30ft nhìn mắt: DC 14 CON. Fail = Restrained → turn tiếp fail = **Petrified**. Fail by 5+ = Petrified NGAY. Averted eyes = không bị nhưng Medusa có Advantage attack
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** 3 attacks (melee or ranged)
  - **Snake Hair.** +5, 4 (1d4+2) Piercing + 14 (4d6) Poison
  - **Shortsword.** +5, 5 (1d6+2) Piercing
  - **Longbow.** +5, range 150/600, 6 (1d8+2) Piercing + 7 (2d6) Poison

> *Petrification = PERMANENT (only Greater Restoration). Avert eyes hoặc dùng mirror.*

## Mind Flayer (Bạch Tuộc Trí Não) (CR 7, 2,900 XP)
- Medium Aberration | Lawful Evil
- **AC** 15 (Breastplate) | **HP** 71 (13d8+13) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 11 (+0) **DEX** 12 (+1) **CON** 12 (+1) **INT** 19 (+4) **WIS** 17 (+3) **CHA** 17 (+3)
- **Saves** INT +7, WIS +6, CHA +6
- **Skills** Arcana +7, Deception +6, Insight +6, Perception +6, Persuasion +6, Stealth +4
- **Senses** Darkvision 120 ft, PP 16
- **Languages** Deep Speech, Undercommon, Telepathy 120 ft
- **Traits:**
  - **Magic Resistance.** Advantage saves vs spells
  - **Innate Spellcasting (INT, DC 15).** At will: Detect Thoughts, Levitate. 1/day: Dominate Monster, Plane Shift (self)
- **Actions:**
  - **Tentacles.** +7, 15 (2d10+4) Psychic. DC 15 INT → **Grappled + Stunned**
  - **Extract Brain.** vs Incapacitated creature grappled: DC 15 INT → **instant kill**, brain eaten
  - **Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6).** 60ft cone, DC 15 INT → 22 (4d8+4) Psychic + **Stunned** 1 min

> *⚠ EXTRACT BRAIN = instant death! Mind Blast AoE stun. Terrifying villain.*
