# Monsters — CR 4 to 5 (XP 1,100–1,800)

> Boss encounters cho party level 3-6. Mid-tier threats.

## Banshee (Ma Nữ) (CR 4, 1,100 XP)
- Medium Undead | Chaotic Evil
- **AC** 12 | **HP** 58 (13d8) | **Speed** 0 ft, Fly 40 ft (hover)
- **STR** 1 (−5) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 10 (+0) **INT** 12 (+1) **WIS** 11 (+0) **CHA** 17 (+3)
- **Saves** WIS +2, CHA +5
- **Resistances** Acid, Fire, Lightning, Thunder; B/P/S nonmagical
- **Immunities** Cold, Necrotic, Poison; Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened, Grappled, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned, Prone, Restrained
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 10
- **Traits:**
  - **Detect Life.** Sense living creatures 5 miles
  - **Incorporeal Movement.** Xuyên qua objects/creatures
- **Actions:**
  - **Corrupting Touch.** +4, 12 (3d6+2) Necrotic
  - **Horrifying Visage.** 60ft, DC 13 WIS → **Frightened** 1 min. Fail 5+ → **Aged 1d4×10 years**
  - **Wail (1/Day).** 30ft, DC 13 CON → **drop 0 HP**. Success = 3d6 Psychic

> *⚠ WAIL = instant dropout! DC 13 CON hoặc 0 HP. Dùng 1/day nên save cho dramatic moment.*

- **AC** 15 (Natural) | **HP** 84 (8d10+40) | **Speed** 30 ft
- **STR** 18 (+4) **DEX** 13 (+1) **CON** 20 (+5) **INT** 7 (−2) **WIS** 9 (−1) **CHA** 7 (−2)
- **Skills** Perception +2
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 12
- **Languages** Giant
- **Traits:**
  - **Keen Smell.** Advantage Perception (smell)
  - **Regeneration.** Hồi **10 HP** đầu turn. Chỉ ngừng nếu nhận **Fire hoặc Acid** damage. Chết thật khi 0 HP + không regenerate

  - **Multiattack.** 1 Bite + 2 Claws
  - **Bite.** +7, 7 (1d6+4) Piercing
  - **Claw.** +7, 11 (2d6+4) Slashing

> *Regen 10/turn = PHẢI có Fire/Acid. Không thì Troll không chết. Party cần Fire!*

## Unicorn (Kỳ Lân) (CR 5, 1,800 XP)
- Large Celestial | Lawful Good
- **AC** 12 | **HP** 67 (9d10+18) | **Speed** 50 ft
- **STR** 18 (+4) **DEX** 14 (+2) **CON** 15 (+2) **INT** 11 (+0) **WIS** 17 (+3) **CHA** 16 (+3)
- **Immunities** Poison; Charmed, Paralyzed, Poisoned
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, PP 13
- **Languages** Celestial, Elvish, Sylvan, Telepathy 60 ft
- **Traits:**
  - **Magic Resistance.** Advantage saves vs spells
  - **Magic Weapons.** Attacks = magical
  - **Innate Spellcasting (CHA, DC 14).** At will: Detect Evil/Good, Druidcraft, Pass without Trace. 1/day: Calm Emotions, Dispel Magic, Entangle

  - **Multiattack.** 1 Hooves + 1 Horn
  - **Hooves.** +7, 11 (2d6+4) Bludgeoning
  - **Horn.** +7, 8 (1d8+4) Piercing
  - **Healing Touch (3/day).** Touch: heal 11 (2d8+2) + cure 1 disease/poison
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Hooves.** 1 hooves attack
  - **Shimmering Shield (2).** +2 AC to creature 1 turn
  - **Heal Self (3).** 11 (2d8+2) HP
