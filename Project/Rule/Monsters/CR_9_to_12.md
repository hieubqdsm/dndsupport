## Aboleth (CR 10, 5,900 XP)
- Large Aberration | Lawful Evil
- **AC** 17 (Natural) | **HP** 135 (18d10+36) | **Speed** 10 ft, Swim 40 ft
- **STR** 21 (+5) **DEX** 9 (−1) **CON** 15 (+2) **INT** 18 (+4) **WIS** 15 (+2) **CHA** 18 (+4)
- **Saves** CON +6, INT +8, WIS +6
- **Skills** History +12, Perception +10
- **Senses** Darkvision 120 ft, PP 20
- **Languages** Deep Speech, Telepathy 120 ft
- **Traits:**
  - **Amphibious.** Breathe air and water
  - **Mucous Cloud.** Creature touch: DC 14 CON → disease: chỉ breathe underwater, 1d4 h ngoài nước = die
  - **Probing Telepathy.** Telepathic contact → biết greatest desire
- **Actions:**
  - **Multiattack.** 3 Tentacle
  - **Tentacle.** +9, 12 (2d6+5) Bludgeoning. DC 14 CON → disease (mucous)
  - **Tail.** +9, 15 (3d6+5) Bludgeoning
  - **Enslave (3/day).** 30ft, DC 14 WIS → **Charmed**. Redo save khi bị damage. Telepathic command
- **Legendary Actions (3/turn):**
  - **Detect.** WIS (Perception) check
  - **Tail Swipe.** 1 tail attack
  - **Psychic Drain (2).** Enslaved creature 30ft: 10 (3d6) Psychic, Aboleth heals same

## Remorhaz (CR 11, 7,200 XP)
- Huge Monstrosity | Unaligned
- **AC** 17 (Natural) | **HP** 195 (17d12+85) | **Speed** 30 ft, Burrow 20 ft
- **STR** 24 (+7) **DEX** 13 (+1) **CON** 21 (+5) **INT** 4 (−3) **WIS** 10 (+0) **CHA** 5 (−3)
- **Immunities** Cold, Fire
- **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft, Tremorsense 60 ft, PP 10
- **Traits:**
  - **Heated Body.** Touch/5ft melee: 10 (3d6) Fire. Nonmagical weapons that hit → destroyed
- **Actions:**
  - **Bite.** +11, 40 (6d10+7) Piercing + 10 (3d6) Fire. Grapple (DC 17). Grappled → Swallow
  - **Swallow.** Swallowed: Blinded, Restrained, 21 (6d6) Acid/turn. Creature deals 30+ from inside → regurgitate

> *40 damage Bite + Swallow + destroys melee weapons on contact. Brutal.*
