# Bard — Thi Sĩ

> Hit Die: **d8** | Primary: **CHA** | Saves: **DEX, CHA**
> Armor: Light | Weapons: Simple, Hand Crossbow, Longsword, Rapier, Shortsword

---

## Tổng Quan

Bard là full caster hỗ trợ tốt nhất: **Bardic Inspiration** buff allies, **Jack of All Trades** giỏi mọi thứ, spell list thiên support/control/healing. Expertise + nhiều skill proficiency = skill monkey xếp hạng cùng Rogue.

**Phù hợp cho**: Người chơi thích RP, support party, và muốn giỏi mọi thứ.

## Class Features Chi Tiết

### Bardic Inspiration (Lv1) — Bonus Action
- BA: cho 1 creature trong 60ft **1 Inspiration die**
- Creature cộng die vào **1 attack roll, ability check, hoặc saving throw** trong 10 phút
- Số lần: CHA mod/Long Rest (Lv5+: PB/Long Rest)
- **Die tăng**: d6 (1) → d8 (5) → d10 (10) → d12 (15)

### Jack of All Trades (Lv2) — Passive
- **+½ Prof Bonus** vào mọi ability check **chưa proficient**
- Áp dụng cả **Initiative** (vì Initiative = DEX check)

### Expertise (Lv2, Lv9) — Passive
- Gấp đôi Prof Bonus cho 2 skills (thêm 2 ở Lv9)

### Countercharm (Lv7) — Action
- Action: allies 30ft → **Advantage** saves chống Charmed/Frightened cho đến end of next turn

### Magical Secrets (Lv10, Lv18) — Passive
- Học **2 phép từ BẤT KỲ class** (Wizard, Cleric, Druid...)
- Lv10: 2 phép. Lv18: 2 phép nữa
- **Game-changer**: Steal Counterspell, Find Greater Steed, Aura of Vitality...

## Build Gợi Ý

### Support Bard (Face of the Party)
- **Stats**: CHA 16, DEX 14, CON 14
- **Subclass**: Lore (Cutting Words + early Magical Secrets)
- **Expertise**: Persuasion, Perception
- **Phép**: Healing Word, Faerie Fire, Hypnotic Pattern, Counterspell (Secret)
- **Chiến thuật**: BA Inspiration/Healing Word. Action: control spells. RP: face mọi social encounter

### Melee Swords Bard
- **Stats**: DEX 16, CHA 16, CON 14
- **Subclass**: Swords (Blade Flourishes + Extra Attack)
- **Weapon**: Rapier (1d8 Finesse)
- **Chiến thuật**: Extra Attack + Flourishes. Medium Armor + Shield = AC respectable

### Lore Controller
- **Stats**: CHA 16, DEX 14, WIS 12
- **Subclass**: Lore (3 extra skills + Cutting Words)
- **Magical Secrets Lv6**: Counterspell + Find Greater Steed / Spirit Guardians
- **Chiến thuật**: Know everything (6+ Expertise skills), counter everything, control battlefield

---

## Tips Cho DM

- Bard **Cutting Words** = Reaction debuff → rất annoying cho monsters
- **Magical Secrets** = Bard gets best spells of all classes → OP at Lv10+
- Jack of All Trades + Expertise = **godlike skill checks** → thiết kế DC phù hợp
- Bard shines ở **RP** → encourage social encounters, performances, persuasion
- **Healing Word** = BA → Bard còn Action để làm thứ khác (Hypnotic Pattern etc.)
