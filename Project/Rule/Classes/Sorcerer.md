# Sorcerer — Phù Thủy Bẩm Sinh

> Hit Die: **d6** | Primary: **CHA** | Saves: **CON, CHA**
> Armor: Không | Weapons: Dagger, Dart, Sling, Quarterstaff, Light Crossbow

---

## Tổng Quan

Sorcerer là full caster với nguồn phép bẩm sinh. Ít phép hơn Wizard nhưng có **Metamagic** để modify phép (twin, quicken, subtle...) — tạo combos độc đáo. **Sorcery Points** cho linh hoạt tạo/đổi spell slots.

**Phù hợp cho**: Người chơi muốn blast damage + combo sáng tạo với Metamagic.

## Class Features Chi Tiết

### Sorcery Points (Lv2) — Special
- **Điểm = Sorcerer level**. Hồi Long Rest
- **Lv5**: Hồi ½ level pts sau Short Rest (1/LR)

**Đổi Points ↔ Slots:**

| Slot Level | Cost (Points → Slot) | Refund (Slot → Points) |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 2 pts | 1 pt |
| 2nd | 3 pts | 2 pts |
| 3rd | 5 pts | 3 pts |
| 4th | 6 pts | 4 pts |
| 5th | 7 pts | 5 pts |

### Metamagic (Lv3) — Special
Chọn 2 (thêm ở Lv10, Lv17):

| Metamagic | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Twinned Spell** | spell level pts | Cast single-target spell lên **2 mục tiêu** |
| **Quickened Spell** | 2 pts | Cast spell = **Bonus Action** |
| **Subtle Spell** | 1 pt | Bỏ **V, S** components (không thể Counterspell!) |
| **Empowered Spell** | 1 pt | Reroll lên đến CHA mod dice damage |
| **Extended Spell** | 1 pt | Duration **×2** |
| **Heightened Spell** | 3 pts | 1 target **Disadvantage** save |
| **Careful Spell** | 1 pt | CHA mod creatures auto **save success** |
| **Distant Spell** | 1 pt | Range **×2** (Touch → 30ft) |

**Combos phổ biến:**
- **Quickened Fireball** → Action còn lại để Firebolt/cantrip
- **Twinned Haste** → buff 2 allies!
- **Subtle Counterspell** → enemy không thể Counterspell lại bạn
- **Heightened Hold Person** → Disadvantage save = dễ fail

> Tankier sorcerer + elemental affinity damage

| Level | Feature | Mô tả |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Draconic Resilience** | +1 HP/level. No armor AC = **13 + DEX** |
| 6 | **Elemental Affinity** | Dragon type spell damage: **+CHA mod**. 1 pt → Resistance |
| 14 | **Dragon Wings** | BA → **Fly speed** = Walk speed |
| 18 | **Draconic Presence** | 5 pts: AoE 60ft Frightened/Charmed (CHA save) |

**Dragon Types:**

| Dragon | Damage Type | Dragon | Damage Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Black/Copper | Acid | Gold/Red | Fire |
| Blue/Bronze | Lightning | Silver/White | Cold |
| Brass | Fire | Green | Poison |

### Wild Magic (Ma Thuật Hoang)
> Chaotic ngẫu nhiên — rolls d100 cho hiệu ứng bất ngờ

| Level | Feature | Mô tả |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Wild Magic Surge** | Cast spell → DM yêu cầu d20. Roll 1 = d100 Surge table |
| 1 | **Tides of Chaos** | Advantage 1 roll. DM reset bằng triggering Surge |
| 6 | **Bend Luck** | Reaction: 2 pts → creature +/- 1d4 on attack/save/check |
| 14 | **Controlled Chaos** | Surge: roll **2 lần** d100, chọn 1 |
| 18 | **Spell Bombardment** | Damage spell: 1 die roll max → reroll 1 extra die add damage |

### Aberrant Mind (Tâm Trí Dị Biệt)

| Level | Feature | Mô tả |

| Level | Feature | Mô tả |
|---|---|---|

## Build Gợi Ý

### Draconic Blaster (Fire)
- **Stats**: CHA 16, CON 14, DEX 14
- **Subclass**: Draconic (Gold/Red → Fire)
- **Metamagic**: Empowered Spell, Quickened Spell
- **Phép**: Burning Hands, Scorching Ray, Fireball
- **Chiến thuật**: Fireball +CHA damage (Elemental Affinity Lv6). Quickened Fireball + Firebolt. Dragon Wings Lv14 = permanent fly

### Twinned Buffer
- **Stats**: CHA 16, CON 16, DEX 14
- **Subclass**: Clockwork (Restore Balance + defensive)
- **Metamagic**: Twinned Spell, Subtle Spell
- **Phép**: Haste (Twinned = 2 allies!), Polymorph (Twin 2 allies into T-Rex), Shield
- **Chiến thuật**: Twinned Haste = 2 allies get extra action. Restore Balance shuts down Advantage/Disadvantage

### Subtle Controller
- **Stats**: CHA 16, CON 14, WIS 12
- **Subclass**: Aberrant Mind (Psionic Spells → no components)
- **Metamagic**: Subtle Spell, Heightened Spell
- **Phép**: Suggestion (Subtle = undetectable!), Counterspell (Subtle = uncounterable), Hold Person
- **Chiến thuật**: Cast spells without ANYONE knowing. Subtle Suggestion in social = OP. Psionic spells save even more Points

## Tips Cho DM

- Sorcerer **ít spells known** → mỗi phép quý giá, respect choices
- **Metamagic** tạo turns cực mạnh nhưng **tốn resources nhanh**
- Subtle Counterspell = **uncounterable** → accept it, nó fair
- Wild Magic = **require DM collab** → roll Surge thường xuyên cho fun
- Twinned Haste: buff 2 allies nhưng **lose Concentration = cả 2 bị stun** → risk
